Gummy

Gummy is a human animal hybrid that lives in a futuristic animal kingdom.Gummy has whiskers the same color as a raging fire and wears a hat piece made by the local forest craftsanimal. He has a thick white fur on his body but doesnt have it on his ears. He is a omnivore that eats anything and his favorite beverage is water from coconuts. His favorite hobby is drawing perfect cirlces on stone rocks and reading folklore about these particularly strange ancient animals called humans.
